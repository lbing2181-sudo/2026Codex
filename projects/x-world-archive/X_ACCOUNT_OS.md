# X Account OS

## Objective

Operate one X account as a premium fictional archive for global English-speaking audiences.

## Account Strategy

- One account
- One supreme universe
- One public seasonal realm at a time
- Many sealed dossiers underneath

## Setup Order

1. lock the handle
2. lock the display name
3. lock the avatar
4. lock the header
5. lock the bio
6. publish 3-7 seed posts
7. wait for profile stability
8. activate Premium
9. add Articles once Premium is active

## Why Premium Matters

- access to Articles
- stronger archive behavior through longform posts
- better operating flexibility for a serious creator account

## Platform Constraint

The project should not depend on immediate X-native monetization from a mainland China entity.
The account can be built now.
Revenue architecture can be designed later through a separate compliant entity path if needed.

## Public Surfaces

### Avatar

Use a symbolic seal, not a human face.

### Header

Use atmospheric worldbuilding, not erotic display.

### Pinned Post

Use the pinned post as the archive gateway.
It should explain the premise, reading order, and active realm.

### Articles

Use Articles for:

- master archive introduction
- season manifest
- realm history
- character index
- ceremonial glossary

## Post Architecture

### Canon Posts

Hero images that define taste and memory.

### Dossier Posts

Character introduction assets with titles, tags, and short lore.

### Fragment Posts

Short lore that deepens the world without requiring a full post package.

### Signal Posts

Questions, polls, alignment prompts, and reactions that turn passive viewers into participants.

### Article Drops

Longform archive entries used sparingly to deepen the canon.

## Publishing Rhythm

Opening phase guideline:

- 2 canon posts per week
- 2 dossier or fragment posts per week
- 1 signal post per week
- 1 article or thread every 2 weeks once Premium is enabled

## Season One Discipline

- limit the first season to 2-3 core women
- keep the setting concentrated
- keep the symbol system consistent
- repeat key words until memory forms

## Growth Thesis

People should follow for the world, not only for the image.
The account wins when users can recognize the archive before seeing the handle.
The account becomes stronger when viewers can recognize the same woman across multiple posts without needing to be told.
