# Account Identity Draft

## Objective

Lock a first-pass X identity that is memorable, premium, and flexible enough to survive future expansion.

## Recommended Direction

The public identity should feel like an archive institution, not a personal creator account and not a single-character fandom page.

## Handle Principles

- short enough to remember
- elegant enough to look premium
- broad enough to contain multiple realms
- not overly fantasy-coded
- avoid numbers unless forced

## First-Pass Handle Candidates

- `@thesablearchive`
- `@sablearchive`
- `@archiveofsable`
- `@sabledossiers`
- `@sealedsable`

## Recommended Display Name

`The Sable Archive`

This should stay stable unless handle availability forces a different naming logic.

## Bio Principles

- one line for the premise
- one line for the archive promise
- no desperate growth language
- no "AI artist" self-label

## Bio Drafts

### Draft A

`Forbidden dossiers from a world of courts, relics, and dangerous women.`

### Draft B

`An imperial archive of perfume, dust, power, and desire.`

### Draft C

`Sealed records from a world where beauty, ritual, and danger share the same throne.`

## Website Field

Leave empty at the beginning unless we create a stable external archive destination.

## Location Field

Prefer blank over misleading.

## Profile Structure Rule

The profile must imply authorship and canon before it implies personality.
