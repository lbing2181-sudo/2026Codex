# Constitution

## Purpose

This project exists to build a premium, English-first, X-native fictional universe that can attract attention, sustain audience desire, and grow into a recognizable body of work.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. The account is a work of authorship, not a prompt dump.
2. All public-facing material must present clearly adult characters only.
3. Sensuality is allowed; explicit sexual display is not the operating strategy.
4. Sexual tension is a structural ingredient, not the sole value proposition.
5. The world must feel expensive, calm, dangerous, and intentional.
6. Every post must belong to one master universe.
7. The first season must stay focused enough to be remembered.
8. Internal documentation must be strong enough for future AI or human handoff.
9. English is the public language of record.
10. Platform survivability matters:
    recommendation health, account trust, and future monetization are part of creative direction.
11. De-AI flavor is mandatory:
    all public output must read as authored, selective, and culturally literate rather than machine-averaged.
12. Character continuity is mandatory:
    any recurring named woman, court, relic, or symbol inside the same universe must preserve a locked identity across images, text, and future AI handoffs.

## Creative Doctrine

- We are building a canon.
- We do not chase random viral aesthetics.
- We do not publish disconnected beauty shots without narrative value.
- We do not dilute the account with too many realms too early.
- We do not mistake erotic noise for artistic magnetism.
- We do not publish text, prompts, images, or videos that feel synthetically padded, generic, or "AI-made" on first contact.
- We do not allow face drift, costume drift, rank drift, palette drift, or symbolic drift to break recurring recognition.

## Audience Doctrine

- The primary audience is Western and global English-speaking users on X.
- The account should feel literary enough to reward attention, but clear enough to spread.
- The audience should feel they are discovering an archive, not consuming disposable content.

## Platform Doctrine

- Public content should remain non-explicit and recommendation-safe whenever possible.
- Highly visible profile surfaces must remain symbolic rather than erotic.
- The account should be built so that Premium, Articles, and later subscription layers can be added without rebranding.

## Memory Doctrine

- Strategic conclusions belong in `DECISIONS.md`.
- Current state belongs in `WORKING_MEMORY.md`.
- New realms, pillars, and format changes must be written back into project files.
