# Avatar System

## Objective

Create a profile identity that is iconic, policy-safe, and legible at small sizes.

## Avatar Direction

Use a symbolic seal instead of a character portrait.

## Recommended Composition

- obsidian black background
- antique gold line work
- one half-closed eye at the center
- one crescent framing the eye
- one keyhole or incense vessel beneath
- one circular heraldic ring enclosing the symbol

## Why This Works

- readable at 48px
- avoids face drift and model inconsistency
- keeps erotic energy indirect
- feels like a dynasty seal rather than a fan account avatar

## Style Rules

- center composition
- minimal detail count
- strong contrast
- no text inside the avatar
- no human body parts
- no explicit sensual imagery

## Header Direction

The header should show atmosphere rather than character detail.

Suggested ingredients:

- palace shadows
- incense haze
- carved arches
- moonlit dust
- distant lanterns
- restrained gold geometry

## First Draft Generation Prompt

`A minimal heraldic emblem on an obsidian black field, antique gold linework, a half-closed eye framed by a crescent, a subtle keyhole and incense vessel motif, circular seal composition, ceremonial, aristocratic, mysterious, high contrast, ultra-clean graphic icon, premium fantasy worldbuilding brand mark, no text, no human figure`

## Practical Build Path

1. generate 6-12 emblem variations
2. select the strongest silhouette
3. simplify further for small-size recognition
4. export square avatar and wide header variants
