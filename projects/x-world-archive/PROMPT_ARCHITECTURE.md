# Prompt Architecture

## Objective

Create a modular prompt system that can expand for years without losing coherence.

## Master Model

Each production prompt should be assembled from layered components rather than written from scratch.

## Core Visual Layers

### 1. Universe Layer

The master archive identity that keeps every image in one canon.

Variables:

- archive tone
- ceremonial density
- era logic
- civilization temperature

### 2. Realm Layer

The specific public or sealed world where the image belongs.

Variables:

- desert court
- velvet metropolis
- nocturne cloister
- salt empire
- brass dominion

### 3. Power Layer

The governing structure around the subject.

Variables:

- court
- temple
- merchant house
- military order
- harbor syndicate
- noble chamber

### 4. Character Archetype Layer

The adult feminine role carried by the subject.

Variables:

- court favorite
- caravan sovereign
- shrine keeper
- masked diplomat
- desert songstress
- widow of rank
- ritual huntress

### 5. Body Presence Layer

The way the body occupies the frame.

Variables:

- poised
- slow
- commanding
- soft but guarded
- predatory calm
- ceremonial stillness

### 6. Expression Layer

The emotional and psychological register.

Variables:

- knowing
- distant
- amused
- merciful
- dangerous
- receptive
- judging

### 7. Sexual Tension Layer

This is a controlled attraction axis, not explicit content.

Variables:

- withheld invitation
- dangerous softness
- devotional gaze
- controlled exposure
- near-touch tension
- dominance through stillness
- surrender implied by posture only

### 8. Wardrobe Layer

The costume system that carries civilization identity.

Variables:

- silhouette
- fabric weight
- transparency level
- jewelry type
- veil logic
- fastening logic
- ornament density

### 9. Gesture and Pose Layer

Body language that implies story.

Variables:

- over-the-shoulder glance
- seated with one knee raised
- veil lifted by two fingers
- hand at throat jewel
- barefoot crossing stone
- leaning against carved column

### 10. Environment Layer

The scene container.

Variables:

- incense chamber
- moon courtyard
- bath arcade
- caravan terrace
- lamp-lit archive room
- wind-cut balcony

### 11. Object and Symbol Layer

Memorable props that increase canon density.

Variables:

- seal ring
- incense bowl
- bronze lantern
- chain veil
- glass vial
- ceremonial dagger
- coded manuscript

### 12. Camera and Finish Layer

The final image discipline.

Variables:

- medium close-up
- full-body portrait
- low angle
- shallow depth of field
- rim light
- gold particulate air
- ultra-detailed
- cinematic
- 4k

## Parallel Operating Layers

These layers do not always appear in the prompt, but they govern the system.

### A. Canon Layer

Which archive class, season, and numbering system this image belongs to.

### B. Platform Layer

How well the image supports X thumbnail recognition and repost behavior.

### C. Safety Layer

Whether the image remains non-explicit and public-facing.

### D. Brand Layer

Whether the image looks authored by `The Sable Archive`.

### E. Serialization Layer

How the image connects to future posts, lore, and character expansion.

## Prompt Assembly Formula

`[master universe] + [realm] + [power structure] + [adult archetype] + [body presence] + [expression] + [sexual tension cue] + [wardrobe system] + [pose] + [environment] + [objects] + [camera/finish]`

## Evaluation Questions

- Is the realm instantly legible?
- Is the woman memorable beyond beauty?
- Is the sexual tension controlled rather than crude?
- Is the wardrobe system specific enough to serialize?
- Does the frame still read at thumbnail size?
- Does the image belong to the archive rather than a random model output?
