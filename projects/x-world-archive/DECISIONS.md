# Decisions

## 2026-04-23

- A new project folder is established for an X-native virtual worldbuilding account.
- The account strategy is work-first and canon-first, not feed-first.
- The public language standard is English-first.
- The primary market is Western and global English-speaking audiences.
- The master universe working name is `Sable Archive`.
- The first public seasonal realm is `The Court of Perfume and Dust`.
- The core emotional spine is `luxury + secrecy + ritual femininity + sexual tension + danger + longing`.
- The opening strategy is one public realm at a time, with other realms kept sealed.
- Public-facing sensuality should remain controlled and non-explicit.
- The avatar strategy is symbolic rather than character-based.
- X-native monetization from a mainland China entity is treated as a future structural problem, not an opening-phase dependency.
- Recurring character consistency is a hard system requirement, not an optional style preference.
