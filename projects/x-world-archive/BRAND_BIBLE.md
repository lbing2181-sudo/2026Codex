# Brand Bible

## Working Master Brand

`The Sable Archive`

This is the provisional master identity for the account and the universe.
It is broad enough to hold multiple realms, characters, eras, and sealed dossiers under one crown.

## Brand Promise

The account offers access to a forbidden archive of women, courts, cities, relics, and rituals drawn from an original fictional universe shaped by luxury, secrecy, desire, and danger.

## Emotional Spine

`luxury + secrecy + ritual femininity + sexual tension + danger + longing`

Every realm, character, and post must carry this emotional spine, even when the setting changes.

## Brand Personality

- controlled
- seductive
- literary
- ceremonial
- aristocratic
- dangerous
- never noisy

## Brand Position

This account should sit between:

- cinematic concept art
- serialized fictional worldbuilding
- premium character mythology
- collectible digital authorship

It must not feel like:

- a random AI image spam account
- low-grade adult bait
- fandom repost culture
- trend chasing with no canon

## Public Identity Layers

### Master Universe

`The Sable Archive`

### Season One

`The Court of Perfume and Dust`

This is the first public release world.
It should establish the taste, language, symbolic system, and female character grammar of the account.

### Sealed Sister Realms

- `The Salt Throne`
- `The Velvet House`
- `The Nocturne Cloister`
- `The Brass Crescent`

These realms are real inside the canon but should remain largely unreleased during the opening phase.

## Content Pillars

1. Character dossiers
2. Archive fragments
3. ceremonial portraits
4. relic and architecture lore
5. coded invitations and whispers

## Brand Test

Before publishing, ask:

- Does this feel like it belongs to one archive?
- Is the sensuality controlled rather than desperate?
- Is the image memorable at both thumbnail size and full-screen size?
- Would a stranger recognize this as authored rather than generated?
