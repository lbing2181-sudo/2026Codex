# PIPELINE

## Objective

Convert a raw client request into a controlled, premium AI commercial output with minimal waste and clear revision logic.

## Standard Flow

1. Intake
   collect product, duration, objective, channel, references, and constraints

2. Direction
   propose 2-3 creative directions and choose one

3. Style Lock
   confirm the image direction before prompt generation

4. Asset Foundation
   generate or define product assets, characters if needed, and brand-world environments

5. Story System
   produce keyframes, grids, and segment logic

6. Video Script
   convert approved visual logic into executable video-generation prompts

7. Revision
   diagnose problems by variable, not by chaos

8. Delivery
   package documents and prompts into a clean client handoff

## Internal Rules

- Product presence must stay strong.
- Style must be locked before production prompts.
- Revisions should change one variable at a time.
- Every output should be prepared for downstream use, not just for display.

## Success Standard

- The client understands the concept quickly.
- The generated visuals hold a consistent world.
- The revision path is controlled.
- The final package is reusable.
