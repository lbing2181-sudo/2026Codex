# OFFER

## Project

`tvc-director`

## Positioning

A high-aesthetic AI commercial film service for real brands.
The focus is not "prompt delivery" but commercial creative output:
creative direction, visual system, asset prompts, storyboard logic, and video-generation scripts.

## Core Offer

- Turn a product or campaign brief into a usable AI commercial production package.
- Deliver outputs that help brands, studios, or creators produce ads faster and cheaper without collapsing taste.

## Target Clients

- Brand owners with visual products
- Small and mid-sized commercial teams
- Creative studios
- Founders who need premium-looking product films without traditional production cost

## Best-Fit Categories

- Automotive
- Fragrance and beauty
- Watches and jewelry
- Consumer electronics
- Premium lifestyle products

## Deliverables

- Creative direction document
- Narrative options
- Art-style recommendation
- Product asset prompt package
- Keyframe / storyboard prompt package
- Video-generation script package
- Revision guidance

## Commercial Principle

- Sell outcome, not tool access.
- Sell clarity, taste, and speed.
- Do not compete on cheap bulk content.
